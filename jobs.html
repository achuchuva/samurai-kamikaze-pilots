Game Animator:

Key information:

Job Summary: Game on! SKP is seeking a highly skilled and creatively-driven video game animator to level up our team. As a video game animator, you'll be the ultimate boss of creating top-notch animations for characters, creatures, and environments in our epic games. You'll collaborate closely with our team of game designers, artists, and programmers to craft a world where every move counts and gameplay is always engaging. So, if you're ready to bring your A-game and join us on our quest for gaming greatness, then let's power up and press start!


Requirements:

Graduate Program:

  1. Bachelor's degree in animation, computer graphics, or a related field
  2. 3+ years of experience in video game animation, including experience with motion capture data and keyframe animation
  3. Proficient in industry-standard animation software, such as Maya or 3ds Max
  4. Strong understanding of animation principles and techniques, including timing, weight, and posing
  5. Experience with game engines such as Unreal Engine or Unity
  6. Strong problem-solving and communication skills
  7. Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
  8. Passion for video games and a desire to create engaging gameplay experiences

Direct Entry:

  1. Portfolio including a showreel, some sketches and concept Art, technical skills. Your portfolio should demonstrate your personal style
